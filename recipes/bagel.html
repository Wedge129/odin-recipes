Bagel

A delicious Montreal breakfast bread

If you're looking for a versatile bread try these out

Ingredients :

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Yeast
  • Sugar

Steps :

  1. Mix the water with the yeast and the sugar
  2. Wait for the yeast to be ready (around 10minutes)
  3. Mix the juicy yeast with the flour and the salt
  4. Wait for an hour and cook the bagel
