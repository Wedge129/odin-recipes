Mexican Rice

A simple recipe for everyone who likes spicey rice

If you're looking for something delicious and quick to make this is a recipe you should try

Ingredients :

  • Rice
  • GroundBeef
  • Onions
  • Spices

Steps :

  1. Cook the ground beef
  2. Remove the beef and cook the onions in the fat
  3. Add the beef with the onions, add diced tomates , beans , and cook the rice in the juice
